ABOUT ME

Hi I'm Jeremy!

I am a third-year Co-op student pursuing a degree in Software Engineering and Management (B.Eng.Mgmt) at McMaster University. Currently, I am a member of McMaster's Satellite Team and contribute to the sponsorship team for McMaster's Hackathon. Additionally, I serve as a Teaching Assistant for Software Engineering classes. You can always find me engaged in side projects, whether they are related to software development or music. Music holds a significant place in my life, and I love playing music with my friends during my spare time.

 My work has primarily centered on the sustainability sector, where my dedication lies in advancing decarbonization efforts. I take pride in actively contributing to sustainable solutions that pave the way for a better future.

Feel free to get in touch if you have any questions. You can find my contact information on my Contact Me page!
